About Us

Established in 1995, SV College has been a pioneer in delivering quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Our faculty is dedicated to mentoring students and fostering a culture of academic excellence and integrity.

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, a well-stocked library, and advanced laboratories. We also emphasize extracurricular activities, encouraging students to explore their talents in sports, arts, and community service.

Our Courses

SV College offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs tailored to meet the demands of the modern job market:

  • 🎓 Computer Science: Learn programming, AI, and software development.
  • 📈 Business Administration: Master the fundamentals of management and entrepreneurship.
  • 🔧 Engineering: Build a strong foundation in core engineering disciplines.
  • 🎨 Arts and Humanities: Explore creativity, culture, and communication.

We also provide specialized certification courses in data science, cybersecurity, and digital marketing to enhance employability.
